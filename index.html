About the Lab

The AI Lab at Portland State University is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of artificial intelligence through innovative research, interdisciplinary collaboration, and real-world applications.

Our lab brings together researchers, students, and industry partners to tackle some of the most challenging problems in AI, from fundamental machine learning algorithms to practical applications that benefit society. We foster an inclusive environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration.

Located in the heart of Portland, we leverage the vibrant tech ecosystem of the Pacific Northwest to bridge academic research with industry needs, ensuring our work has meaningful impact.

Research Areas

Our research spans multiple domains of artificial intelligence

Reinforcement Learning

We build sample-efficient and robust RL agents across model-free and model-based settings, with a focus on world models, efficient representations, and online adaptation for long-horizon decision-making. We also study robust multimodal RL and how generative/foundation models can strengthen RL in real-world applications (e.g., anomaly detection, recommendation).

Generative AI

Our work centers on generative representation learning, especially VAEs and their modern variants (hierarchical VAEs, VQ-VAEs), to learn compact latent spaces for compression, robustness, world models, and anomaly detection. We also work on GAN-based robustness, diffusion models, and LLM-guided reward shaping in RL.

View Generative AI Projects
üéØ

Applied Machine Learning

We develop applied ML systems in geospatial, computer vision, and health domains, including GeoAI for drainage-structure detection from LiDAR DEMs and multispectral pedestrian detection for nighttime safety with robustness/bias analysis. We also use interpretable ML to study subgroup-specific drivers of quality of life in autistic adults in an NIH R01/CBPR collaboration.

Our Mission & Goals

01

Advance AI Research

Conduct cutting-edge research that pushes the boundaries of artificial intelligence and contributes to the global knowledge base.

02

Educate Future Leaders

Train the next generation of AI researchers and practitioners through mentorship, coursework, and hands-on research experience.

03

Foster Collaboration

Build partnerships with industry, government, and other academic institutions to accelerate innovation and knowledge transfer.

04

Address Real-World Challenges

Apply AI solutions to solve pressing problems in healthcare, climate, education, and other critical domains.

05

Promote Ethical AI

Ensure our research and applications prioritize fairness, transparency, accountability, and societal benefit.
